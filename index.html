Biography

I am currently a first-year M.S. student with the iCAS Lab at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, under the supervision of Prof. Xinfei Guo. I received my B.Eng. degree in Information Engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2025.

My research focuses on electronic design automation (EDA), particularly physical design for 2.5D and 3D integrated circuits, including placement and floorplanning optimization.
